Traditional Washoku:
The Art of Japanese Home Cooking

Dive deep into the philosophy of Japanese cooking — from dashi-based broths and knife skills to the delicate balance of umami in every dish. This comprehensive 24-lesson course takes you from beginner fundamentals to confident Japanese home cooking.

KT
Chef Kenji Tanaka
Executive Chef, 25 years experience · Tokyo & Paris
24 lessons 8 hours

What You'll Learn

By the end of this course, you'll have the confidence to prepare authentic Japanese dishes from scratch — no shortcuts, no packets. Every technique is grounded in centuries of tradition, explained clearly for the modern home cook.

  • The five fundamental tastes (go-mi) and how to balance them in every dish
  • Dashi preparation from kombu and katsuobushi — the backbone of Japanese cooking
  • Proper knife technique: the santoku, gyuto, and the art of the razor-sharp edge
  • Rice washing, seasoning, and steaming to achieve sushi-restaurant quality
  • Essential sauces: teriyaki, ponzu, miso, and gomadare
  • Mise en place methodology adapted for the Japanese kitchen

Course Curriculum

  • Module 1: The Japanese Pantry (2 lessons)
  • Module 2: Knife Skills & Cutting Techniques (4 lessons)
  • Module 3: Dashi & Soup Bases (3 lessons)
  • Module 4: Rice & Grain Mastery (3 lessons)
  • Module 5: Vegetable Preparations (4 lessons)
  • Module 6: Fish & Seafood (4 lessons)
  • Module 7: Meat & Poultry (3 lessons)
  • Module 8: Plating & Presentation (1 lesson)
